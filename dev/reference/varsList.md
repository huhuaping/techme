# Basic vars table

A dataset containing all variables and additional information, such as
unit, chn_name, eng_name etc.

## Usage

``` r
varsList
```

## Format

A data frame:

- variables:

  price, in US dollars

- chn_full_name:

  price, in US dollars

- short_chn:

  price, in US dollars

- short_eng:

  price, in US dollars

- units:

  price, in US dollars

- block1:

  price, in US dollars

- block2:

  price, in US dollars

- block3:

  price, in US dollars

- block4:

- chn_block1:

  price, in US dollars

- chn_block2:

  price, in US dollars

- chn_block3:

  price, in US dollars

- chn_block4:

  price, in US dollars

- chn_full:

  price, in US dollars

- source:

  price, in US dollars
