# Details Data Frame of China Agricultural Research System (CARS)

A data set containing detail informations of China Agricultural Research
System (CARS) from the public site MOA <http://www.moa.gov.cn>, with
wide data format.

## Usage

``` r
PubCars
```

## Format

A data frame:

- year:

  character, year marked in the file name

- index:

  character, ordered index number

- area_num_eng:

  character, encoding of Agri-product industry with digits 2

- area_name:

  character, name of Agri-product industry

- chairman_industry:

  character, chairman of the industry

- institution_industry:

  character, institution of the chairman for certain industry

- func_num:

  character, encoding of functional research area with digits 2

- func_name:

  character, name of functional research area

- func_inst:

  character, institution of the director in functional research area

- func_director:

  character, name of director in functional research area

- researcher_area:

  character, detail research direction witin functional research area

- researcher_name:

  character, name of researcher

- researcher_inst:

  character, institution of the esearcher

- province:

  matched province of the institution
